Me, Myself and I

Born in 1996 in Agadir, Morocco. Hello! My name is Esma and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2021 when I decided to watch some HTML and CSS tutorial videos on Youtube. Turns out I found my new passion.

What excites me most about working in web development is being able to design and create things that have purpose and solve real problems.

Well-organised person, problem solver, independent employee with high attention to detail. Fan of soccer, outdoor activities, TV series and reading. Interested in the entire frontend spectrum and working on ambitious challenging projects with positive people.

I'm open to Job opportunities where I can contribute, learn and grow. If you have a good opportunity that matches my skills and experience then don't hesitate to contact me.
